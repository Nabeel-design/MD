Athletic Sports Medicine Center at OSU

This fast track project supports clinical sports medicine skills development and physical therapy services and also provides diagnostic and treatment services. Located adjacent to Oregon State University’s Reser Stadium, the facility provides teaching space for OSU research scientists and engineers. Collaboration opportunities for academic faculty and sports medicine physicians is a fundamental basis for the program.

Physical Activity

The national guidelines for physical activity states that for health benefits adults should engage in at least 30 minutes a day of moderate intensity activity on 5 days a week. There are numerous AIT sports teams, clubs and societies based around physical activity. Look out for our annual Clubs and Societies Day as an opportunity to check out what is on offer.
